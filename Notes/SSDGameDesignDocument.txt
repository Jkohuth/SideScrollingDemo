Overview - Hack and Slash platformer with branching paths that lead to the same destination.

Theme / Setting / Genre - Isolation, will to power, growth through struggle. Northern European inspired landscape with unique enemies and a few taken from existing Germanic/Romanic Mythos. Hack and Slash Platformer 

Core Gameplay Mechanics Brief
        2D Platforming
        Hack And Slash Combat
        Wall Movement for verticality
        Rail Grinding - Speed/Platforming Challenges
        Updrafts - Verticality

Targeted platforms
        PC
        Switch (Dream On)
Project Scope
        As this will be my first project followed to completion my only goal is a single level

Influences (Brief)
        - Ninja Gaiden (NES) - Original Hack and Slash platformer
        - Castlevania (NES) - Enemy placement and overall level design
        - Sonic 2  (Genesis) - Branch linear levels
